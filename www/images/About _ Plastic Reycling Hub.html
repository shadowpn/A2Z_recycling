    About Us

    Plastic Recycling Hub is born from the founding company Delta Plastic. Over three years now, Delta Plastic has found its feet as a community based plastic recycling hub, recycling plastics that others find challenging. Our mantra is resources over revenue, focusing on turning unskilled individuals into professional operators enabling us to expand our capability of numerous plastics.

     

    The birth of Plastic Recycling Hub was established from a team of passionate individuals who had a vison to make an impact on the environment and obtain an economy that keeps ANZ manufacturing in ANZ. Our strong and determined team consists of 20+, operating 24 hours for 5.5 days a week.

     

    Plastic Recycling Hub will be expanded into Melbourne, Brisbane, Newcastle and Canberra. Partnering with Bingo Bins, Cleanaway, Remondis and Veolia, we strive towards supporting Australia – in particular, Sydney-based businesses – with providing solutions, tools and services that are required for waste collection and recycling.
